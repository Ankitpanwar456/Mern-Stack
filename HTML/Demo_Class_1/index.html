About Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

31 October 1875 – 15 December 1950), commonly known as Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was an Indian independence activist and statesman who served as the first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of India from 1947 to 1950.
